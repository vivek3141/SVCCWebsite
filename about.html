About Us

Welcome to our Silicon Valley Cubing Club! Our goal for this club is to provide a friendly environment for kids interested in Cubing to gather together and learn from each other. We have leaders who specialize in 3x3 cubes, but are also extravagant in many more kinds of cubes! At this club, we will host competitions for kids that already know how to solve a 3x3 rubiks cubes, with fun prizes for the winners. If you or your child is still unfamiliar with a rubiks cube, don't worry, we love teaching kids who are new and interested! So come over to SVCC!
