You Can Solve it Too!

The Rubik's Cube doesn't require insane math skills, or great memory. Here at SVCC, you can do the cube too! Come join us at the club and get ready to have some fun. All you need to bring is a cube, a minimum of three brain cells, and your enthusiasm.
